Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Shared Notes (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the App.

Shared Notes is operated by the developer of the Notes4Couples project.

Information We Collect

The App collects only the information necessary to provide its core functionality, which may include:

  • Account information (such as email address and user identifier)
  • User-generated content (notes, categories, and related metadata)
  • Authentication data required to maintain secure sessions
  • Basic technical information (such as device type and operating system)

How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the App’s features
  • Synchronize notes across devices
  • Enable collaboration and sharing
  • Maintain security and prevent unauthorized access

Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks. These providers may collect limited information such as advertising identifiers, in accordance with applicable laws and their own privacy policies.

The App does not sell personal data to advertisers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. Information is shared only when necessary to operate the App securely, deliver advertisements, or when required by law.

Data Storage and Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect your data. All data is encrypted in transit. While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Account and Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and all associated data at any time. Deleted data is permanently removed within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law.

Account deletion requests can be submitted at:

https://notes4couples.com/delete-account

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
